  37   *
  1. Implement this interface and its methods.

  38   *

    3. 
  39   * it will be notified of exceptions thrown while attempting to

  41   *

    4. 
  42   *    method.

  44   * A listener that wants to be notified of OBJECT_CHANGED event types
  45   * should also implement the ObjectChangeListener
  46   * interface.

  48   * @author Rosanna Lee
